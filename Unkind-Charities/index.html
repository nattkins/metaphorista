Unkind Charities

19-12-16

This year metaphorista is illustrating just some of the charities that you can give you time and money to.

The Mephistopheles Fund

Another year has passed and his work all around the world has gone unacknowledged, from crucial work overseeing elections, to peace keeping in such places as Yemem and Syria or to his lesser known work in the sciences and health care sectors he has tirelessly sought to bring general agreement that there be distrust and resentment between groups of all ages, creeds and religions. All these works done with a wide variety of stake holders, interested parties and pressure groups on all sides of our divided world. Please help him in any way you can, any donation no matter how small minded is appreciated. If you post just one hateful tweet then it all goes to help. A regular donation of ill informed bile is a gift that benefits the most, and can really make a big difference.

Serious Voice

The Unloved Post

This Christmas give a thought to those posts that no one reads, posts that record third place in a running race for your second oldest that didn’t even get one view. All those posts that urgently asked people to do stuff and no one did a damn thing. Please help in anyway you can by randomly clicking on all the posts that you see.

Concerned voice.

V D Benevolent Fund

Our primary aim is to support venereal diseases who are at risk of being made irrelevant, are irrelevant or otherwise could be totally forgotten , or have experience of being forgotten and are trying to establish or maintain a new outbreak. A grant from the VDB can remove the threat of cure.
Give a thought to those diseases like the Aguee or Imposthume once feared by millions young and old alike now relegated to history books and left in forgotten petri dishes in museum laboratories. But what can you do? it only takes a thoughtless act to give these diseases and many more like them a new lease of life; new fangled fads like washing your hands and clean food preparation are wide spread today and you can do your bit to leave food out to be nibbled by your pets. Regular cuts and graves if left unattended and allowed to rester can really provide a reliable source of help to these forgotten diseases, this is the best way to say that you care. Give all you can.
