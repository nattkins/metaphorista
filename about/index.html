What is this about

What are you doing here?

This is where we investigate together the magic that is metaphor and its power as a problem solving tool.

How will you do this?

Good question - by which I mean I’m not entirely sure, but suffice to say that by sharing metaphors and light may be thrown on your problem solving dilemmas.

A metaphor is not a dream.

And by dream here I mean the being-asleep type dream not the rhetorical Martin Luther King type dream. Because when someone waking up after having a particularly ‘good’ dream the chances are, that it will loose almost everything in the telling.

A metaphor is a dream.

A Metaphor is like a dream with its clothes on and out the door ready to get involved in life, and such is the sort of thing that will increase in value for being shared.

OK OK lets have an example

Yesterday - 6th December 2016 on BBC Radio 4 a program entitled “The life scientific” Jim Al-Khalili discussed with Richard Morris how memory works and they used the metaphor of “Walking across a field of deep grass and trampling down the grass” as being like the laying down of long term memory. And here is where things getting interesting - that long term memory needs, as it were, for the grass to be trodden down just adjacent to it, what they called a “recall cue” ,and then we can find ourselves back on the path of a particular recalled memory. It is exactly what actors have known all along, that if they dry up in a speech a little cue of only few words can bring the whole speech flooding back to their recollection.

listen again to the whole interview

Now that is exactly what this site will hope to be a gallery of - metaphors that might help you to think with.
